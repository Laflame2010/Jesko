THE LEGACY

BEGINS

From a dream in 1994 to automotive excellence today

1994 - THE VISION

Christian von Koenigsegg was just 22 when he watched "The Pinchcliffe Grand Prix" and decided to build the perfect sports car. What started as a young man's dream would become one of the most respected hypercar manufacturers in the world.

2002 - THE FIRST ROAR

After 8 years of development, the Koenigsegg CC8S was born. With a Ford V8 engine producing 655 horsepower, it announced Koenigsegg's arrival in the hypercar world. Only 654 examples were ever made, making it an instant classic.
