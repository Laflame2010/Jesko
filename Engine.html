THE POWERHOUSE

The Jesko's 5.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine is a marvel of modern engineering. Developed entirely in-house by Koenigsegg, this powerplant delivers an earth-shattering 1,600 horsepower on racing fuel, making it one of the most powerful production engines ever created.

Advanced technologies like the revolutionary Light Speed Transmission (LST) and sophisticated turbocharger system ensure that this immense power is delivered with precision and reliability. The engine features a flat-plane crankshaft, titanium connecting rods, and a compression ratio optimized for both performance and efficiency.

ENGINEERING PERFECTION

Every component of the Jesko's engine has been meticulously crafted to achieve the perfect balance of power, efficiency, and reliability. From the carbon fiber intake manifold to the custom-designed exhaust system, no detail has been overlooked in the pursuit of automotive excellence.
